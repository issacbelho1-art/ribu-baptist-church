About Our Church

Faith. Hope. Love in Christ

Who we are

Ribu Baptist Church is committed to sharing the Gospel of Jesus Christ with faithfulness and love. Our ministry focuses on teaching the Word, strengthening families, and serving the community with compassion.

Mr. Taulang Phom

Mr. Taulang Phom faithfully leads the church with a strong commitment to preaching the Word of God. His passion for ministry, prayer, and spiritual growth helps guide the congregation in truth and unity.

Mrs. Taulang Phom

Mrs. Taulang Phom plays a vital role in supporting the ministry through prayer, encouragement, and care for families and women. Her life reflects grace, strength, and dedication to serving God and the church community.

We believe in one God — Father, Son, and Holy Spirit. The Bible is the inspired Word of God, and salvation comes through faith in Jesus Christ. We are called to live in love, holiness, and obedience.

We are a family united in Christ. Everyone is welcome here. Together we worship, grow in faith, and serve the community with love and purpose.

Our Children & Youth Fellowship is a space where young people grow together in faith, learning, and creativity. Through our activities, we nurture both spiritual and personal development.

We run a special program where children and youth receive help with their school studies while also learning Sunday school lessons and biblical teachings. Alongside this, they participate in choreography, dance, and singing gospel songs.

This initiative is open not only to our church members but also to children from other communities, creating a welcoming and loving environment for all.
