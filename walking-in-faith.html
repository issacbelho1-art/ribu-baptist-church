Walking in Faith

Faith is trusting God even when we cannot see the path ahead. In our daily lives, we often face uncertainty, challenges, and moments where answers are not clear.

But faith reminds us that God is always in control. He sees what we cannot see, and He is working even when we feel nothing is happening.

As believers, we are called to walk not by sight, but by faith. Every step we take with Him strengthens our trust and deepens our relationship with Him.

“For we walk by faith, not by sight.” – 2 Corinthians 5:7

Today, take one step forward in faith. Even if you are unsure, trust that God is guiding your path.
